Friction Stir Welding

IIT Dhanbad
  • Conducted a simulation of friction stir welding using ANSYS. The problem was modeled as a 3D multiphase non-Newtonian flow with the Sheppard-Wright shear stress model
  • Observed the formation and evolution of inter-metallic compounds from the simulation, results concur with prior experiments
  • Quantified the effect of mechanical properties of the joint by the tool offset and welding speed, based on analysis of inter-metallic compounds
  • Also worked extensively on image processing techniques to distinguish Copper from Aluminum in FE-SEM renderings, which resulted in a publication in the journal 'Measurement'
August 2017 - May 2020

Study of Mechanical Outages in a Coal Washery

Tata Steel Ltd.
  • Studied the mechanical outages at BCPP(Bhelatand Washery) for the last 3 years and determined the root causes and recommendations for reduction of outage by 20%
  • After detailed analysis of provided data and regular plant visits, many improvements in operation, maintenance and management were suggested, complying to the methods and objectives followed in TPM and CBM
  • Theorized and formulated a prototype for 'Condition Based Monitoring using Recurrent Neural Networks' employing long short term memory (LSTM) units coupled with high fidelity sensor array for fault prediction in critical components
  • Received an employment offer from Tata Steel as Management Trainee
