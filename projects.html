Study of Mechanical Outages in a Coal Washery

Tata Steel Ltd.

Studied the mechanical outages at BCPP(Bhelatand Washery) for the last 3 years and determined the root causes and recommendations for reduction of outage by 20%. After detailed analysis of provided data and regular plant visits, many improvements in operation, maintenance and management were suggested, complying to the methods and objectives followed in TPM and CBM. I theorized and formulated a prototype for 'Condition Based Monitoring using Recurrent Neural Networks' employing long short term memory(LSTM) units coupled with high fidelity sensor array for fault prediction in critical components. I was offered a pre-placement offer from Tata Steel.
